Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI skin analysis work?

Our AI analyzes your selfie to identify skin concerns like redness, dryness, irritation, and texture issues. It then provides personalized recommendations based on your unique skin profile.

Is my photo data secure?

Yes! Your photos are processed securely and encrypted. We never share your photos with third parties. You can delete your photos anytime from the app settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime through your App Store account settings. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Aureya and tap "Cancel Subscription".

Why are certain products recommended for me?

Product recommendations are based on your skin type, concerns, and sensitivities you identified in your skin profile. Our AI matches you with products formulated for sensitive skin.

Can I use this app if I have a skin condition?

Aureya provides general skincare guidance. If you have a diagnosed skin condition like eczema, rosacea, or psoriasis, please consult with your dermatologist before trying new products.

How often should I do a skin analysis?

We recommend doing a skin analysis every 2-4 weeks to track your skin's progress and adjust your routine accordingly.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data from the app's Profile > Settings section. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
